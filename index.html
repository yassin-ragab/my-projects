Our Legacy

Where Egyptian Creativity Takes Shape

The Faculty of Applied Arts at Helwan University is one of Egypt's most prestigious institutions for creative and design education. Rooted in the rich cultural heritage of the Nile civilization, we bridge timeless artistic traditions with the demands of the modern world.


Since our founding, we have produced thousands of designers, artists, and innovators who have shaped Egypt's visual culture — from product design and fashion to ceramics, textiles, and interior architecture.

What We Stand For

Built on Four Pillars

Our institution is guided by values that have shaped generations of Egyptian creatives and continue to define our academic culture today.

  • 🔥
    Passion for Craft

    We cultivate deep love for the craft in every student, believing that true mastery comes from genuine enthusiasm for one's art form.

  • 🌍
    Cultural Roots

    Egypt's 7,000-year artistic heritage is the backbone of our curriculum, inspiring students to create work that is both global and deeply Egyptian.

  • 💡
    Innovation First

    We push students to experiment, fail, and push boundaries, creating a culture where originality is celebrated over conformity.

  • 🤝
    Community Impact

    Our graduates go on to transform Egypt's creative industries, design landscape, and cultural institutions, leaving a lasting legacy.
